Activities

The Laughing Club

As they say 'Laughter is the Best Medicine'
so laughter is important in every person's life,
hence we have this Club where all the elderly people laugh
everyday in the morning to begin their morning with happiness.

Chess Matches

Chess is a Mind Game and hence it is very good for the Brain.
These mental benefits improve social and physical health and improve quality of life.

Painting

Painting helps in regualar activity of arms and hands of old people
and it promotes better blood flow and dexterity.
Hence it is important for old people .
Painting activities are held twice a week for all.

Handbag Making

While some people love to paint some people love stitching,
So to make people explore what they love we have machines where the stitch
different variety of handbags, pouches and clutches.

Handmade Mask

During Covid'19 Mask has been a necessity and wearing colourful or embroidered
mask has been a fashion style and is also worn for safety purposes.
Hence mask making has been made the new trend.

Carpet Making

Regardless of everthing carpets will always be in trend , from making carpets to rugs
we have all kinds of varities made by our own very talented
people , who have amazing skills.
